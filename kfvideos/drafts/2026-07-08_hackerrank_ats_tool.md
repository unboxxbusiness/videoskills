## 2. Audience Psychology
* **Kyun dekha students ne?** Most students face instant rejections from job portals without knowing why. The ATS tool is a "black box" they want to understand.
* **Kyun ruke?** They want to know how the tool parses resumes and how they can use it to test their own resume.
* **Kyun share kiya?** To help classmates who are actively applying for off-campus internships or jobs.
* **Kyun comment kiya?** "How to run this tool on my resume?", "Link for the github repo please!"
